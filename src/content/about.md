### Hi, I'm Andrew 👋

I've been photographing the world for as long as I can remember. I grew up in the Bay Area on the Peninsula, where I first picked up a camera as a kid and started capturing whatever caught my eye. Over time, photography became less about what I was seeing and more about who I was with.

I've never done this for money. It has always been something personal, something I do for my family. From my parents and siblings to the life I've built with my wife and our two kids, my camera has always been a way to hold onto the moments that matter. My first camera was a Canon 70D, but what really shaped me was the feeling of preserving time in a way that words never could.

Now living in Arizona, that purpose has only grown stronger. I want my kids to be able to look back at these photos one day and see their childhood as it truly was. I believe photographs are more than just images — they are something real to hold onto, something that lasts longer than memory alone.
